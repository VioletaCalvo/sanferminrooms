We offer a 3 bedroom apartment (one double room and two double -single rooms), conveniently located in Pamplona’s center. The apartment is completely furnished consisting on a fully equipped kitchen including larder, a bathroom and a living room. There is the possibility of using extra beds and a sofa bed. It is composed of two balconies. One of these is an outside balcony and the other one is an inside one. (They are not overlooking the bullrunning race) It has a capacity of 6 to 8 people, on a total surface of 92 m2. The flat is on a second 2º floor without elevator.

The apartment is only 10 minutes walking from the first stretch of the bullrunning race. The so called “Santo Domingo slope”. From there you can take Pamplona´s elevator to the historic city center (the old quarter) which is a 30 seconds ride and does not pay any fee.
